Brief Biography

My name is Caden Perdue, I was born in Lexington, NC on March 27 th 1997. I went to SWES until I was in 3rd grade, then my family moved to Winston-Salem, North Carolina and I started attending Midway Elementary School. We lived a happy suburban life in a fairly nice neighborhood where I filled my days riding bikes and running through the woods with my friends.

I eventually moved up to NDMS where I began to cultivate my interest in computers and how they actually worked. I took an elective class there where I was introduced to robotics, flight simulators, and other various pieces of technology that captured my interest. It was at this point in my life that I realized I wanted my future to be centered around some kind of science or engineering.

By the time high school came around I found myself spending my nights playing video games with my friends, and drinking lots of H2O. By the end of high school it had become very clear to me that I wanted to study Computer Science for the rest of life. In 2015 I decided to further my education at Appalachian State University where I am currently a senior.

Career Goals

As for the future of my career, I would, of course, like to apply my Computer Science skills to my job. For now my main goal is to simply find somewhere to work, preferably somewhere relatively close by so that I don't have to make a huge move to a new state. After a few years of saving money and plannig my future I hope to find a job in Cyber Security or Web Development somewhere out west. Ideally I will immediately find a company that I have a strong drive to work for where I will be able to move my way up the ladder and become an expert in my field.
